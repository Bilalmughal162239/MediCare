Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department offers comprehensive heart care with state-of-the-art technology and experienced specialists.

The department is equipped with advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities to provide the best care for patients with heart conditions. Our team of cardiologists, cardiac surgeons, and specialized nurses work together to deliver personalized treatment plans.

Services Offered

Cardiac Consultation
Electrocardiogram (ECG)
Echocardiography
Stress Testing
Cardiac Catheterization
Angioplasty & Stenting
Pacemaker Implantation
Cardiac Rehabilitation

Neurology Department

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system.

The department provides comprehensive care for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our team of neurologists and neurosurgeons use advanced diagnostic tools and innovative treatments to address complex neurological disorders.

Services Offered

Neurological Consultation
Electroencephalography (EEG)
Electromyography (EMG)
Nerve Conduction Studies
Stroke Management
Epilepsy Treatment
Headache & Migraine Care
Movement Disorder Treatment

Orthopedics Department

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders of the musculoskeletal system.

The department provides comprehensive care for conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Our team of orthopedic surgeons and specialists use advanced techniques and innovative treatments to address complex orthopedic issues.

Pediatrics Department
