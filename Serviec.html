Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our website, by calling our appointment line at +1-800-MEDICARE, or by visiting our reception desk in person. For urgent cases, we also offer same-day appointments.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department for specific information about your insurance coverage.

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer secure video consultations for many of our services. Telemedicine appointments can be scheduled through our website or by calling our appointment line. These virtual visits are covered by most insurance plans.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical records from previous providers (if available), and any relevant imaging or test results. Arriving 15 minutes early to complete paperwork is recommended.

How do I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. If you haven't registered yet, please visit our reception desk with your ID to get set up. For printed copies, please submit a medical records request form.
